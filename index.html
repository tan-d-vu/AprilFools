Who are we?

Guided outdoor adventure sports are a $12.9B industry in the US, but the world still lacks a comprehensive, trustworthy marketplace for guides to connect with clients online. Adventurelist seeks to solve that problem by creating a single place where the adventurously-minded can connect with qualified, certified experts who will lead them on their next great expedition.

We strive to connect outdoor adventurers of all skill levels and abilities with exciting, meaningful outdoor experiences.

Newcomer looking to learn? Experienced adventurer? Weekend warrior?

Adventurelist is the place for you.

Our Values

Accessibility

Adventurelist believes in democratizing access to America's outdoors. No matter your background, skillset, or ability level, Adventurelist wants to help you find meaningful experiences in the great outdoors.

Conservation

We know that introducing more people to the wilderness comes with the responsibility to steward our natural resources. All Adventurelist adventurers and guides must pledge to leave no trace and maintain the outdoors as pristine as we found it.

Learning

We know that a skilled adventurer is a safe adventurer. With that in mind, we strive to offer learning experiences to make you safer and more confident in your outdoor pursuits.
