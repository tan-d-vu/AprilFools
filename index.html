About Us

  • A Passion for Adventure

    Like you, we love to get outdoors but hate spending time searching, calling, and booking guides.

  • What We Do

    Adventurelist is an online marketplace and community where guides can list their trips so you can search, discover, and book them easily. We make adventure simple.

  • Our Goal

    We strive to connect outdoor adventurers of all skill levels and abilities with verified, credentialed guides across outdoor industries to create exciting, meaningful outdoor experiences.

  • Adventures for All

    Adventurelist is for anyone who wants to get outside. We offer instruction for newcomers, opportunities to learn new skills, and treks for the experienced outdoor enthusiast!

Our Core Values

Accessibility

Adventurelist believes in democratizing access to America's outdoors. No matter your background, skillset, or ability level, Adventurelist wants to help you find meaningful experiences in the great outdoors.

Conservation

We know that introducing more people to the wilderness comes with the responsibility to steward our natural resources. All Adventurelist adventurers and guides must pledge to leave no trace and maintain the outdoors as pristine as they found it.

Learning

We know that a skilled adventurer is a safe adventurer. With that in mind, we strive to offer learning experiences to keep you safer and help you be more confident in your outdoor pursuits.
